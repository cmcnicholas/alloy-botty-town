Simple Sky

Offset the UV's on the SimpleSky material to change time of day.

HOW TO ALTER NIGHT AND DAY CYCLE:
1. Import the Asset
2. Open the Folder: _Prefabs
3. Drag SkyDome prefab into your scene
4. Click on the SkyDome parent object
5. In your Inspector go to Mesh Renderer component then Materials
6. Double-click the SimpleSky material
7. Now edit the Offset X value in order to change from Night and Day
